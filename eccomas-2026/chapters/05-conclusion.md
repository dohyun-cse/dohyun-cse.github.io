# Conclusion

Treating the box constraint $0\le\rho\le 1$ as part of the geometry —
rather than as an obstacle to project against — gives a cleaner
iterate sequence (cf. [[03-mirror-descent^thm-conv]]) and a simple
convergence theory. Open directions include (i) anisotropic entropies
for multi-material designs and (ii) stochastic mirror descent for
large-scale problems [[@bib-beck]].
