I received the Ph.D. degree in Computational Science and Engineering from Yonsei University, Seoul, Republic of Korea, in 2021. My thesis advisor was Professor Eun-Jae Park.

I'm particularly interested in discontinuous Galerkin (DG) for fluid problems.
Currently, I'm working on residual type error estimator for the Euler equations. I'm also interested in PDE constrained optimization problems.
